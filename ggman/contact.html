Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EpicGameZone

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Just click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of the page, fill in your details, and you'll be ready to start playing.

Are the games free to play?

Yes, all games on EpicGameZone are completely free to play. Some games may offer optional in-game purchases for additional features or cosmetics.

Can I play games on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and most games are optimized for mobile play. You can enjoy your favorite games on smartphones and tablets.

How often are new games added?

We add new games every week. Make sure to check our "New Releases" section regularly or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.

Is there a way to suggest a game?

Yes! We welcome game suggestions from our community. You can use the contact form on this page to send us your ideas.
